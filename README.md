As a medical doctor with an MPH and passion for digital health, I combine clinical expertise with advanced analytics to extract meaningful insights from health data.

## Projects

### 1. Cardiovascular Disease Risk Prediction
**Description:** Machine learning model to predict cardiovascular disease risk based on patient demographics, clinical indicators, and lifestyle factors.

**Techniques:**
- Logistic Regression
- Random Forest Classification
- Feature Engineering
- Cross-validation

**Key Findings:**
- Achieved 87% accuracy in risk prediction
- Identified key risk factors: age, blood pressure, cholesterol levels
- Developed interpretable model for clinical decision support

[View Project](./cardiovascular-risk-prediction)

### 2. HIV Treatment Adherence Analysis
**Description:** Analysis of factors affecting antiretroviral therapy adherence using data from a Nigerian HIV treatment program.

**Techniques:**
- Survival Analysis
- Cox Proportional Hazards Model
- Time-to-Event Analysis
- Data Visualization

**Key Findings:**
- Identified predictors of treatment discontinuation
- Developed risk stratification tool for patient support
- Informed program improvements achieving 90% adherence rate

[View Project](./hiv-adherence-analysis)
